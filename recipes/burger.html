Burger

Description

Need a basic burger recipe that you can whip up in no time? We have the perfect one. With just 5 ingredients and 15 minutes of cooking

time, this flavorful recipe is ready to be shared, topped, and sauced in any way you like! Learn how to make your own sizzling beef burger patties right here

Ingredients

  • egg
  • salt
  • beef
  • black pepper
  • bread crumbs

step by step

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Add ground beef
  4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill.
